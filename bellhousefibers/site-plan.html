Blog

A Knitter's Kit Everything you need to be the best knitter you can be. Here is a list of essential items to have on hand so you can learn to knit with confidence. -A comfy chair -Good lighting -Reading glasses or a magnifier -Written pattern instructions (If you want to learn how to read a picture pattern click here) -Neddles- Double Pointed or Circular (A note about neddles: use one size larger than what the pattern states if you are a tight knitter.) -Practice Yarn- (This yarn does not have to be the expensive stuff. Use yarn that is thicker than usual becuase you will be able to see the stitches better. I recommend not using mohair to practice a swatch or new pattern becuase it is very fine and hard to tink out.) -Scissors -Music or your favorite show on in the background -Determination that you can do it. (Knitting is fun and relaxing and the best part is a beautiful wearable creation when your finished.)
